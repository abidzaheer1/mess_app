Why Alpha Mess

Everything your housemates need in one app

💰

Smart expenses

Log groceries, utilities, and meals with receipt photos. See weekly spending charts and recent activity at a glance.

📊

Monthly settlement

Equal-split math done for you. Know your share, who owes the admin, and export a PDF report for the group.

💬

Mess chat

Text messages and hold-to-record voice notes. Unread badges so nobody misses an update.

🛒

Grocery rotation

Daily duty rotation, swap requests with accept/decline, and reminders for whoever is on duty today.

🎉

Events

Plan outings or group meals, track estimated cost per person, and attach event expenses.

🔔

Live notifications

New expenses, chat messages, join requests, and swap alerts — delivered while you use the app.

👥

Members & invites

Invite codes, admin approval for join requests, and role management for your mess.

🔐

Secure sign-in

Email, Google sign-in, strong passwords, and forgot-password recovery via Firebase.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

  1. 1Create or join a mess with an invite code
  2. 2Add expenses as they happen — snap a receipt optional
  3. 3Chat, rotate grocery duty, plan events together
  4. 4Review monthly settlement and settle with the admin
